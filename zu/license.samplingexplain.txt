<p>Ilayisensi Yokuthatha Isampula ikuvumela ukuba umeme abanye abantu ukuba basebenzise ingxenye ethile yomsebenzi wakho futhi bayenze ibe yintsha.</p>

<p>Ngokwesibonelo:</p>

<ul>
<li>Ukuthatha isampula engomeni yakho bayifake kweyabo.</li>
<li>Ukusebenzisa ingxenye ethile yefilimu yakho.</li>
<li>Ukuthatha ingxenye ethile yesithombe sakho bayifake eqoqweni elithile.</li>
</ul>
