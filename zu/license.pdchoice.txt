<p>Lokhu okukhethile kusikisela ukuthi ufuna ukunikezela umsebenzi wakho ekutheni usetshenziswe umphakathi noma ikanjani, idlelo lokwaziswa nenkulumo lapho kungekho okungokothile futhi yonke into ivumelekile.</p>

        <p>Ukunikezela Imvume Emphakathini akuyona ilayisensi. Ngokusebenzisa lokhu, awusuke nje umane ushintsha okuthile elungelweni lakho lobunikazi; unikezela lonke ilungelo lakho lobunikazi emphakathini ngokungenamibandela. Lokhu kulinikezela kuhlala njalo futhi akuhlehliseki.</p>
