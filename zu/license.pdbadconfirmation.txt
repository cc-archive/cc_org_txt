<h1>Ukunikezelwa Emphakathini - Isiqinisekiso Esibi</h1>

<p>
Kubonakala sengathi uzama ukuqinisekisa ukunikezela kwakho emphakathini
ngekhodi yesiqinisekiso okungeyona.</p>

<p>
Cishe lokhu kwenzeke ngoba awukwazanga ukuchofoza
i-URL ethunyelwe ku-imeyili yakho, wabe usukopisha futhi wanamathisela i-URL
ngokungeyikho kwisipheqi. Qiniseka ukuthi ufaka <b>zonke
izinhlamvu</b> ze-URL oyithunyelelwe.
</p>

<p>
Uma kungekona lokhu okwenzekile, noma uma uqhubeka unenkinga
yokuqinisekisa ukunikezela kwakho emphakathini nganoma iyiphi indlela, sicela <a
href="mailto:pd@creativecommons.org">usazise</a>.
</p>
