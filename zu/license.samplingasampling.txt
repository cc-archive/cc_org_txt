<strong>Ukuthatha Isampula Nokunye</strong>: Abantu bangase bathathe futhi bashintshe <strong>izingxenye</strong> ezithile zomsebenzi wakho ngenjongo ethile ngaphandle kokukhangisa, okuyinto eyenqatshelwe. <strong>Ukukopisha</strong> nokunikeza abanye (njengokuhlanganyela amafayela) umsebenzi wonke <strong>kodwa ungenzeli ukuthola inzalo</strong> nakho kuvunyelwe. Yingakho kuthiwa, "<strong>nokunye</strong>".
