<p>The Sampling licenses let you invite other people to use a part of your work and make it new.</p>

<p>For example:</p>

<ul>
<li>To take a sample from your song and include it in their own.</li>
<li>To use a clip from your film.</li>
<li>To take a piece of your photograph and put it into a collage.</li>
</ul>

<p>Think of it as a way to reward people for transforming your work into something it wasn't before.</p>
